Built with p5.js.

Controls:

  • Click in the canvas to put down and pick up the dot.
  • Move the mouse to control the position of the dot (when picked up).
  • Double-click or press P to toggle pattern fill.
  • Mousewheel UP or press + to increase grid mesh.
  • Mousewheel DOWN or press - to decrease grid mesh.
  • You can save the image as you would any image. (No need to screenshot.)
