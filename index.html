Coordinates

Visualizing a plane in Cartesian and circular-polar coordinates.

Built with p5.js.

Controls

  • Click in the canvas to put down and pick up the dot.
  • Move the mouse to control the position of the dot (when picked up).
  • Double click or press P to toggle pattern fill.
  • Mousewheel UP over the canvas, or press + (or =), to increase grid mesh.
  • Mousewheel DOWN over the canvas, or press -, to decrease grid mesh.
  • You can save the image as you would any image.
    So no need to screenshot—though that works too.
